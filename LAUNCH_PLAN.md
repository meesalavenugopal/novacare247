## Executive Summary

NovaCare is a 24/7 physiotherapy clinic platform connecting patients with expert physiotherapists across India. The platform offers clinic visits, home visits, and video consultations, making quality physiotherapy accessible to everyone.

## 2. Launch Strategy

### 2.1 Soft Launch (Week 1-2)

**Objective:** Test systems with limited users

- Launch in 1-2 cities (Hyderabad + Vizag)
- Invite-only access for friends & family
- Collect feedback and fix issues
- Monitor booking flow end-to-end
- Test payment processing

### 2.2 Beta Launch (Week 3-4)

**Objective:** Controlled public access

- Open registrations with waitlist
- Offer 50% discount for first 100 bookings
- Intensive customer support
- Daily monitoring and bug fixes
- Collect video testimonials

### 2.3 Public Launch (Week 5+)

**Objective:** Full market entry

- Press release to local media
- Influencer partnerships activated
- Full marketing campaign launch
- All cities operational
- 24/7 support active

2. **Verification** - License, experience check (48 hrs)

## 9. Risk Mitigation

### 9.1 Risk Matrix

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low doctor supply | Medium | High | Aggressive onboarding, incentives |
| Competition | High | Medium | Differentiation, quality focus |
| Technical issues | Low | High | Robust testing, backup systems |
| Regulatory changes | Low | High | Legal compliance, industry associations |
| Payment fraud | Medium | Medium | Fraud detection, secure gateway |
| Bad reviews | Medium | High | Quality control, quick resolution |

### 9.2 Contingency Plans

**If bookings are low:**
- Increase marketing spend
- Offer deeper discounts
- Partner with more corporates
- Add free initial consultations

**If doctor churn is high:**
- Reduce commission rates
- Add doctor benefits
- Improve payment terms
- Create community/events

**If competition intensifies:**
- Focus on niche (24/7, home visits)
- Improve technology
- Build brand loyalty
- Consider strategic partnerships
